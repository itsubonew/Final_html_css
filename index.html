How does this even work?

We hear you.Get hands on with the Me luna cup.

You have questions,
We have answers.

Our Me luna Cup Model 1 holds one full ounce of menstrual flow (30 ml) and Model 2 holds just over one ounce (32 ml).
the average regular sized tampon holds about 5 ml of menstrual flow.

Before your first cup use, submerge the DivaCup in an open pot of boiling water for 5 – 10 minutes, using a stainless steel pot that is designated to boil your Me luna Cup.
This is a mandatory first step.

Using the Me luna Cup does require you to get a little more up close and personal with your flow, but it is clean and comfortable to use.
Be sure to wash your hands well before inserting and removing, as well as after you’re finished reinserting.
